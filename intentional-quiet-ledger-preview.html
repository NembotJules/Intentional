Intentional / fresh design direction

Quiet Ledger for your attention.

Intentional should feel like a private record of effort, not another productivity arcade. The system is warm, exact, and calm: plan the day in light, enter focus in dark, leave with proof.

Design thesis Your day becomes accountable to your pillars. The UI should make attribution obvious, leakage visible, and focus feel like a real commitment.
